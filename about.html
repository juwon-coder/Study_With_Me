What is Study_with_Me?

Study_with_Me is a study matching platform that helps learners connect and grow together. You can find study groups in various fields and create your own to build an efficient learning experience.

Our goal is to create a community where all learners can find their optimal study partners, achieve their learning goals, and grow by positively influencing each other.

Key Features

Diverse Categories

You can search and participate in studies across a wide range of fields, including language, coding, certifications, job searching, academics, and hobbies/liberal arts.

Easy Study Creation

You can easily create a study group and become a leader by simply entering a few pieces of information.

Intuitive Search and Filtering

Quickly find desired studies using various conditions such as keywords, categories, progress methods, and recruitment status.

Recruitment Status Check

You can check the recruitment status of studies at a glance and apply to participate.

How to Use?

1. Browse Studies

Search for studies of interest on the home screen or study list page and check the details.

2. Apply to Join a Study

If you find a study you like, click the 'Apply to Join' button to express your interest.

3. Create a New Study

On the 'Create Study' page, you can create a new study group by entering the study leader's name, study name, category, description, and more.

4. Manage My Studies

On the detailed page of a study you created, 'Edit' and 'Delete' buttons are displayed, allowing you to manage study information.

Future Plans

In the future, we plan to provide richer features such as user-specific profile management, in-study chat functionality, and automatic recruitment count updates.
